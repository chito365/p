<span style="font-family:Verdana;">Common modeling elements<br />Entities – represent customers, parts, messages, paperwork, airplane, etc.<br />Attributes – Information stored with each entity<br />Usually, every individual entity has the same set of attributes, but the values differ to distinguish the entities<br />Some attributes are automatic, others are user-defined and user-maintained<br />Resources – servers, machines, workers, nodes, links, runways, gates, agents, clerks, etc.<br />Queues – where entities wait if resources are not available</p>
